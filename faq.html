Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before your first session.

Wear whatever is comfortable for you. During the massage, you'll be covered with towels and only the area being worked on will be uncovered. Most clients undress to their level of comfort, and I'll always step out of the room while you get settled on the table.

Sessions are available in 30, 60, and 90-minute options depending on the treatment. Your first appointment includes an additional 10–15 minutes for a consultation, so please arrive a few minutes early to allow time for paperwork and a brief chat about your needs.

Massage should never be unbearable. Some deeper techniques may cause mild discomfort as tight muscles are released, but I always work within your comfort level. Communication is key — if the pressure ever feels too much, just let me know and I'll adjust straight away.

This depends on your individual needs and goals. For general relaxation and maintenance, once a month works well. If you're dealing with chronic pain, recovering from an injury, or managing high stress levels, more frequent sessions — every one to two weeks — may be beneficial. We'll discuss a plan that suits you during your consultation.

Yes, I offer pregnancy massage for clients who are past their first trimester. The treatment uses specially adapted techniques and positioning to keep you comfortable and safe. Please let me know about your pregnancy when booking so I can prepare everything you need.

Your first visit begins with a short consultation where I'll ask about your medical history, lifestyle, and what you'd like to achieve from the treatment. This helps me tailor the session to your specific needs. After that, you'll get settled on the table and your massage will begin — there's nothing to worry about.

No, you don't need a GP referral to book a massage. However, if you have a specific medical condition or are recovering from surgery, I may recommend checking with your doctor first to make sure massage is appropriate for you. Safety always comes first.

I understand that plans change. I ask for at least 24 hours' notice if you need to cancel or reschedule your appointment. Late cancellations or missed appointments may be charged at the full treatment rate, as I hold that time exclusively for you.

Massage is safe for most people, but certain conditions may require medical clearance or a modified approach. During your consultation, I'll ask about your health history so I can adapt the treatment accordingly. If you're unsure, please get in touch before booking and I'll be happy to advise.

Yes, gift vouchers are available for any treatment or monetary amount. They make a lovely gift for birthdays, anniversaries, or just to show someone you care. Get in touch to purchase a voucher and I'll have it ready for you to collect or send directly to the recipient.
