Your Visit

What to Expect

Your first appointment is straightforward and relaxed. Here's how a typical session works from start to finish.

1

Consultation

We'll start with a chat about your needs, health history, and any areas of concern. This helps me understand your body and choose the right approach.

2

Assessment

I'll assess your posture, range of movement, and areas of tension to identify where your body is holding stress and what needs attention.

3

Your Treatment

A tailored massage session using the techniques best suited to you. I'll check in throughout to make sure the pressure and focus are exactly right.

4

Aftercare

I'll share practical tips on stretching, hydration, and self-care to help you maintain the benefits of your treatment between sessions.
