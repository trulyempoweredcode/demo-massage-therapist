What's Included in Every Treatment

Every session begins with a brief consultation so I can understand your needs and tailor the treatment accordingly. Clean towels and linens are provided, and the treatment room is always warm, private, and welcoming.

After your massage, I'll share personalised aftercare advice — including stretches, hydration tips, and self-care suggestions — to help you get the most from your treatment between sessions.

Payment is accepted by card, bank transfer, or cash. Gift vouchers are also available for any treatment — a thoughtful way to treat someone you care about.
