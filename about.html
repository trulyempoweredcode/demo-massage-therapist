About Me

Hello, I'm Emma

I'm a qualified massage therapist based in the heart of Cheltenham, and I've been helping people move better, sleep better, and feel more at home in their bodies for the past eight years. I trained at the London School of Massage, where I fell in love with the science of how touch can genuinely transform how someone feels — physically and emotionally.

I specialise in both remedial and relaxation massage, which means whether you're dealing with a stubborn shoulder from months of desk work or you simply need an hour to properly switch off, I can help. Every session I deliver is tailored to what your body needs on that particular day.

When I'm not in the treatment room, you'll usually find me hiking somewhere in the Cotswolds, unrolling my yoga mat for an early morning flow, or experimenting with a new recipe in the kitchen. I'm a firm believer that looking after yourself isn't a luxury — it's essential.

My Journey

From Office Chair to Treatment Table

A decade in marketing left me with chronic back pain and a growing feeling that I was built for something different. One massage changed everything.

For ten years I worked in marketing — fast-paced agencies, long hours hunched over a laptop, and a constant low-level ache across my shoulders and lower back that I just accepted as normal. I tried painkillers, I tried ignoring it, I tried a fancy ergonomic chair. Nothing really shifted it.

Then a friend persuaded me to try a remedial massage. Within an hour, the therapist had identified tension patterns I'd been carrying for years and started to release them. I walked out of that session feeling lighter than I had in months. More than that, I finally understood that the pain wasn't something I had to live with — it was my body asking for help.

That experience changed the course of my life. I enrolled at the London School of Massage, completed my ITEC Level 3 Diploma, and went on to gain specialist qualifications in sports massage, pregnancy massage, and hot stone therapy. Eight years on, I've treated hundreds of clients from my treatment room at The Willows in Cheltenham — and I still get the same deep satisfaction from helping someone walk out feeling better than when they arrived.

My Approach

How We'll Work Together

From your first enquiry to ongoing care, here's what the journey looks like.

1

Free Chat

Get in touch by phone or email and we'll have an informal conversation about what's going on, what you're hoping for, and whether massage therapy is the right fit. No pressure, no commitment — just an honest chat.

2

First Session

Your first appointment is a 75-minute session that includes a thorough consultation. I'll ask about your health history, lifestyle, and any areas of concern before tailoring a treatment specifically to your body.

3

Treatment Plan

After your first session, I'll recommend a treatment plan based on what I've found. This might be a short course of focused sessions for a specific issue, or regular maintenance to keep your body feeling its best.

4

Ongoing Wellbeing

Many of my clients come back regularly — not because they're in pain, but because they've discovered how much better they feel when massage is part of their routine. I'll support you for as long as you need.
