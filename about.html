About Lauren

Helping You Feel Your Best

I became a massage therapist because I believe everyone deserves to feel comfortable in their own body. Whether you're in pain, stressed, or simply in need of some time to switch off, I'm here to help.

About Me

Hello, I'm Lauren

I'm an ITEC-qualified massage therapist based in Leamington Spa, and I've spent the last eight years helping people get out of pain, manage stress, and take better care of their bodies. Before I retrained, I worked in a busy office environment — long hours at a desk, constant tension in my neck and shoulders, and a growing sense that something needed to change.

A sports massage after a half-marathon was the turning point. The therapist didn't just ease the pain — she explained what was happening in my body and showed me how to look after it properly. That single experience sparked something, and within a year I'd enrolled on my ITEC Diploma. I haven't looked back since.

When I'm not treating clients, you'll find me running along the canal, experimenting with new recipes in the kitchen, or dragging my reluctant terrier on walks through Jephson Gardens.

My Approach

I believe massage therapy works best when it's truly personal. No two bodies are the same, and no two sessions should be either. Before every treatment, I take the time to understand what's going on — not just the symptoms, but the habits, postures, and stresses that might be contributing.

I combine techniques from Swedish massage, deep tissue, sports therapy, and aromatherapy depending on what your body needs that day. Some sessions are about deep, focused work on a stubborn knot. Others are about switching off your nervous system and giving your body permission to rest.

My goal is always the same: to help you leave feeling better than when you arrived, and to give you the knowledge and tools to look after yourself between sessions.

Credentials

Training & Qualifications

Your safety and wellbeing are my priority. Here are the qualifications and certifications behind my practice.

ITEC Level 3 Diploma

ITEC Level 3 Diploma in Massage Therapy — internationally recognised qualification covering anatomy, physiology, and professional massage techniques.

Sports Massage

Certified in sports massage therapy — advanced techniques for injury prevention, rehabilitation, and performance support for athletes at all levels.

Pregnancy Massage

Specialist training in pregnancy massage — safe, gentle techniques adapted for each trimester to relieve discomfort and promote relaxation during pregnancy.

Aromatherapy

Certified aromatherapist — trained in the therapeutic use of essential oils to enhance massage treatments and support physical and emotional wellbeing.

Fully Insured

Full professional indemnity and public liability insurance — giving you complete peace of mind every time you step into my treatment room.

Ongoing CPD

Committed to continuous professional development — regularly attending courses in myofascial release, trigger point therapy, and hot stone techniques.

Your Journey

How a Session Works

From the moment you arrive to the aftercare advice you take home, here's what a typical visit looks like.

1

Arrive & Settle

My treatment room is warm, quiet, and private. You'll have time to settle in, change, and let the outside world fall away before we begin.

2

Discussion

We'll talk through how you're feeling, any areas of pain or tension, and what you'd like to get from the session. First visits include a full health consultation.

3

Treatment

Your massage — tailored to your body and your needs. I'll adjust pressure and technique throughout, and you're always welcome to give feedback during the session.

4

Aftercare

Afterwards, I'll share stretches, hydration tips, and self-care advice to help you get the most from your treatment and keep your body feeling good between visits.
