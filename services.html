What I Offer

Treatments

Every body is different, and every treatment I give reflects that. Whether you need gentle relaxation, focused deep work, or specialist support during pregnancy, I offer a range of massage therapies tailored to your needs. All treatments take place in my calm, private therapy room in central Cheltenham.

Swedish Massage

Swedish massage is the foundation of everything I do and the treatment most people think of when they picture a classic massage. Using long, flowing strokes combined with gentle kneading and rhythmic tapping, I work across the whole body to ease muscular tension, boost circulation, and calm your nervous system. It is a beautifully effective treatment for anyone feeling run down, stressed, or simply in need of an hour to switch off and let their body unwind.

This is the treatment I recommend most often for first-time clients, because it gives your body the chance to relax deeply without any intense pressure. Many of my regular clients come back for Swedish massage month after month as part of their ongoing wellbeing routine. You will leave feeling lighter, looser, and genuinely rested — the kind of rest that a good night's sleep alone cannot always provide.

  • Full-body relaxation using flowing effleurage strokes
  • Improves blood circulation and lymphatic drainage
  • Reduces stress hormones and promotes deep calm
  • Ideal for first-time massage clients
  • Eases headaches, fatigue, and general muscle stiffness

Deep Tissue Massage

Deep tissue massage is designed for those stubborn areas that a lighter touch simply cannot reach. Using slow, deliberate pressure and targeted techniques, I work into the deeper layers of muscle and fascia to break down adhesions, release chronic knots, and restore proper movement. This is the treatment I turn to most for clients who sit at desks all day, carry tension in their shoulders and neck, or have long-standing postural issues that cause recurring pain.

I always communicate with you throughout a deep tissue session to ensure the pressure is firm enough to be effective but never beyond your comfort level. Some tenderness during treatment is normal, but it should always feel productive rather than painful. Many clients tell me they feel a noticeable difference after just one session — and with regular treatment, those persistent tight spots and nagging aches can become a thing of the past.

  • Targets chronic muscle tension and deep-seated knots
  • Breaks down scar tissue and fascial adhesions
  • Relieves desk-related neck, shoulder, and back pain
  • Improves range of motion and postural alignment
  • Pressure always tailored to your comfort level

Hot Stone Massage

Hot stone massage combines the deeply soothing warmth of smooth, heated basalt stones with hands-on massage techniques to create one of the most luxurious and therapeutic treatments I offer. The stones are carefully heated to the perfect temperature and placed along key points of the body — your spine, shoulders, and palms — while I use additional stones as an extension of my hands to massage away tension. The penetrating warmth relaxes muscles far more quickly than hands alone, allowing me to work more deeply with less pressure.

This treatment is particularly wonderful during the colder months or for anyone who tends to feel the cold and holds tension because of it. The heat draws blood flow to the surface, warming you from the inside out while easing stiff joints and sore muscles. Clients often describe it as the most deeply relaxing massage they have ever experienced. If you are looking for something that feels truly indulgent while still delivering real therapeutic benefits, hot stone massage is the one to try.

  • Heated basalt stones placed along key energy points
  • Penetrating warmth relaxes muscles at a deeper level
  • Increases blood flow and eases joint stiffness
  • Perfect for cold weather or those who carry tension from the cold
  • Combines luxury with genuine therapeutic relief

Sports Massage

Sports massage is not just for athletes — it is for anyone with an active lifestyle or a body under physical demand. Whether you run, cycle, swim, lift weights, or simply spend your weekends on your feet, sports massage helps your muscles recover faster, perform better, and stay injury-free. I use a combination of deep tissue work, stretching, and trigger point therapy to target the specific muscle groups that your activity puts under strain, breaking down tension and restoring full range of movement.

I work with everyone from weekend park runners to competitive athletes, and I understand that the needs of each person are different. Pre-event massage focuses on warming up and preparing your muscles for performance, while post-event treatment concentrates on flushing out waste products and reducing soreness. For ongoing maintenance, regular sports massage keeps your body in peak condition and catches small issues before they become injuries. If something does not feel right, I will always advise honestly and refer you on if needed.

  • Pre-event, post-event, and maintenance treatments
  • Speeds up muscle recovery and reduces soreness
  • Trigger point therapy for stubborn muscular pain
  • Helps prevent injury and maintains peak performance
  • Suitable for all fitness levels, not just elite athletes

Pregnancy Massage

Pregnancy puts extraordinary demands on your body, and massage can be one of the most supportive things you do for yourself during this time. I am trained in specialist pregnancy massage techniques that are safe and comfortable from the second trimester onwards, using careful positioning with supportive cushions so you can fully relax without any pressure on your bump. Treatment focuses on the areas that pregnancy affects most — your lower back, hips, legs, and shoulders — relieving the aches that come with carrying extra weight and the postural changes your body goes through.

Beyond the physical relief, many of my pregnant clients tell me that their sessions are the one hour in the week where they truly feel cared for and able to let go. Pregnancy massage can help reduce swelling in the legs and ankles, ease sciatic pain, improve sleep quality, and lower the stress and anxiety that sometimes accompany pregnancy. I create a calm, nurturing space where you can feel completely safe, and I always adapt my approach as your body changes through each stage of your pregnancy.

  • Safe and comfortable from the second trimester onwards
  • Relieves lower back, hip, and sciatic pain
  • Reduces swelling in legs and ankles
  • Improves sleep quality and reduces anxiety
  • Specialist positioning with supportive cushions for comfort
