Our Treatments

Massage Therapies

Every treatment is tailored to your needs. Whether you're looking for deep relaxation, pain relief, or recovery support, I'll work with you to find the right approach for your body.

Swedish Massage

The classic relaxation massage. Swedish massage uses long, flowing strokes, gentle kneading, and rhythmic tapping to ease tension and calm the nervous system. It's the ideal choice if you're new to massage or simply want to unwind after a stressful week.

This full-body treatment improves circulation, encourages lymphatic drainage, and leaves you feeling lighter and more at ease. I adjust the pressure throughout to suit your comfort level, so every session feels just right.

Benefits

  • Reduces muscle tension
  • Improves circulation
  • Promotes deep relaxation
  • Boosts immune function

Deep Tissue Massage

Deep tissue massage targets the deeper layers of muscle and connective tissue using slow, deliberate strokes and firm pressure. It's particularly effective for chronic aches, tight spots, and postural problems that build up over time.

This treatment works to break down adhesions and scar tissue, restoring normal movement and reducing pain. While the pressure is firmer than a Swedish massage, I always work within your comfort zone and check in throughout the session.

Benefits

  • Breaks down adhesions
  • Relieves chronic pain
  • Improves posture
  • Reduces inflammation

Sports Massage

Sports massage is designed for athletes and active individuals who want to train harder, recover faster, and stay injury-free. Whether you're a weekend runner or a competitive athlete, this treatment focuses on the muscle groups you use most.

I use a combination of deep tissue techniques, stretching, and trigger point therapy to address tightness, improve range of motion, and speed up recovery. Sports massage is equally effective as a pre-event warm-up or a post-event recovery treatment.

Benefits

  • Speeds up recovery
  • Prevents injuries
  • Improves flexibility
  • Enhances performance

Hot Stone Massage

Hot stone massage combines the therapeutic benefits of heat with skilled massage techniques. Smooth, heated basalt stones are placed on key points of the body and used as an extension of my hands to melt away tension and stiffness.

The warmth from the stones penetrates deep into the muscles, allowing me to work more effectively without the need for heavy pressure. It's a wonderfully soothing treatment that calms the mind while easing physical discomfort.

Benefits

  • Deeply relaxing
  • Eases muscle stiffness
  • Improves blood flow
  • Calms the nervous system

Pregnancy Massage

Pregnancy massage is a safe, gentle treatment designed to support expectant mothers from the second trimester onwards. As your body changes, aches and discomfort in the back, hips, and legs are very common — and massage can make a real difference.

I use specially adapted positioning and techniques to ensure you're completely comfortable throughout. This nurturing treatment eases the physical strains of pregnancy while giving you a well-deserved moment of calm before your baby arrives.

Benefits

  • Eases back and hip pain
  • Reduces swelling
  • Improves sleep quality
  • Relieves anxiety

Aromatherapy Massage

Aromatherapy massage combines the power of touch with the therapeutic properties of pure essential oils. Each blend is carefully chosen to address your specific needs — whether that's calming an anxious mind, lifting low mood, or soothing sore muscles.

The oils are diluted in a carrier oil and applied with gentle, flowing strokes that allow them to absorb through the skin while you breathe in their natural fragrance. It's a deeply holistic treatment that works on both body and mind.

Benefits

  • Elevates mood
  • Soothes headaches
  • Supports emotional balance
  • Nourishes the skin
